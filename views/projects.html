Bus Companion


Bus Companion is a web app that provides users the following functions on their smartphones:

  • Bus Search The user can enter their source, destination of travel and the timing in the application which will then suggest the bus number and nearest bus timing to that specified by the user.
  • Online Booking This module will allow the user to book a ticket for the bus of their choice by paying online through net banking, debit card details, Paytm etc.
  • RFID Cards Regular users of the transport system will be issued RFID cards with a balance in them obtained on payment of that amount.
  • Media Content and Live Location The users will be offered various media content once they have boarded the bus and have connected to the bus server. They can also use the live location feature implemented using GPS module.
