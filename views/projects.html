Bus Companion


Bus Companion is a web app that provides users the following functions on their smartphones:

  • Bus Search The user can enter their source, destination of travel and the timing in the application which will then suggest the bus number and nearest bus timing to that specified by the user.
  • Online Booking This module will allow the user to book a ticket for the bus of their choice by paying online through net banking, debit card details, Paytm etc.
  • RFID Cards Regular users of the transport system will be issued RFID cards with a balance in them obtained on payment of that amount.
  • Media Content and Live Location The users will be offered various media content once they have boarded the bus and have connected to the bus server. They can also use the live location feature implemented using GPS module.

Vehicle Speed Detection


The project uses OpenCV on Python to detect cars and detect their speeds in real time. It uses a Haar cascade to detect cars and triangular similarity to detect their real time speeds from the video feed. We can also use a pre recorded video file as input to detect speed of cars in it. It is a great application of the image processing tools such as OpenCV and triangular similarity. It can help you solve traffic issues and speeding promblems on the roads in your neighbourhood.

Mano-Mouse Gesture Control


You are watching a movie with your friends on a laptop and one of the guys gets a call.Ahh.. you have to get off your place to pause the movie. How better would it be if you could control your mouse from wherever you were? Well, we have a solution! Mano. Simply speaking, a camera captures your video and depending on your hand gestures, you can move the cursor and perform left click, right click, drag, select and scroll up and down. Hotel Recommendation System

Such systems are increasingly popular in recent years, and utilized in variety of areas of market. For the recommendation system to work various criteria like previous browsing history of user, purchases, cost preferences etc. are considered. For the development of our project-Hotel Recommendation system, we have used data from two sites- Foursquare and Yelp. Image Steganography with Visual Cryptography


Steganography is the science of writing hidden data in such a way that no one, apart from the sender and intended recipient, suspects the existence of the message. Images are made up of pixels. Each pixel represents 3 bytes: Red, Green and Blue. The difference between two colors that differ by one bit in either one red, green or blue value is impossible to detect for the human eye. Therefore, we can change the least significant bit in a byte. This means, we can overwrite the last bit in a byte without affecting the colors. Decryption of the data can be done by subtracting the original image with cover image, leaving behind the encrypted data. Over Image Steganography, we will implement a Visual Cryptography algorithm. Visual cryptography is a technique that allows information (such as text, images, etc.) to be encrypted using encoding system that can be decrypted by the eyes. The basis of the technique is the superposition of two (or more) semi-transparent layers. Individually, there is no message printed on either one of the sheets. Overlapping them creates addition interference to the light passing through.
